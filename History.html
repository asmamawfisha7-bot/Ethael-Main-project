Welcome to Ethael Website Development Academy

LEADING PRIVATE WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT SCHOOL IN ETHIOPIA

Ethael website development Academy began as a training center, offering specialized short-term programs to address industry demands. With a commitment to excellence, it expanded its academic offerings and evolved into a Tutor service , later earning full Trainning center. Today, Ethael website development Academy stands as a premier institution, dedicated to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and empowering students for professional success. Through continuous growth and adaptation, it remains a leader in higher education, shaping the future of learners across various disciplines.







2018 Ethael Website Development was founded

Ethael website Acadamy was founded in October 2018, initially operating as the "Ethael Website Tutor." The Training Centre began by delivering specialized, six-month short-term programs, catering to the evolving training needs of the community.

2019 Transitioning to the status of a Full Tutor.

In April 2019, the Training Centre underwent a significant upgrade, transitioning to the status of a Tutor. This marked the beginning of a period of steady growth and development. The institution continued to expand its offerings and enhance its resources, both human and material.

2020 Accredited as a "Trainning center."

By March 2020, the Tutor center had reached a new milestone, being accredited as a "Trainning center. " This elevated status allowed EWD to broaden its academic scope and solidify its position as a leading provider of higher Trainning center.

2021 Full accreditation as a "Acadamy"

After seven and a half years of distinguished service as a Trainning center, Ethael Website Development Trainning center achieved a remarkable feat in July 2021. The institution was granted full accreditation as a "Acadamy" by the Ministry of Education of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia. Since its establishment, Ethael website Development Academy has graduated tens of thousands of students across various fields of study, including web development, Android Development, and Software Engineering. The Acadamy's campuses are strategically located, with six campuses in Addis Ababa Abado, megenagna, mexico, Ayat, Kazanchiz, Piazza and bolee. In its ongoing pursuit of expanding quality services to the community, Ethael Website Development Acadamy has extended its reach beyond the borders of Ethiopia. As Ethael website Acadamy continues to grow and evolve, its commitment to excellence and its unwavering dedication to serving the educational needs of its students remain at the core of its mission. The university's remarkable journey serves as a testament to its resilience, innovation, and the profound impact it has had on the lives of countless individuals and communities.
